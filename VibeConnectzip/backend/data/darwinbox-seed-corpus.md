# Darwinbox Comprehensive Knowledge Base

## Company Overview
Darwinbox is an AI-native, cloud-based Human Capital Management (HCM) and Human Resource Management System (HRMS) platform founded in 2015. It automates the hire-to-retire employee lifecycle for medium and large enterprises. Darwinbox was founded by Chaitanya Peddi, Jayant Paleti, and Rohit Chennamaneni; Vineet Singh was elevated to Co-Founder and CTO in 2024. The founding HQ is Hyderabad, India and the global HQ is Singapore. Darwinbox became a unicorn in January 2022 and serves 1,000+ enterprises, 4 million+ employees, and 130+ countries.

## Product Positioning
Darwinbox positions itself as a mobile-first, AI-native, enterprise-grade HCM suite with global and local compliance, no-code configurability, open REST APIs, and lower total cost of ownership. Its vision is to build a People OS for the entire workforce, from frontline workers to the CEO. Its tagline is "Technology for your People."

## Product Architecture
Darwinbox is a unified, modular, multi-tenant SaaS HCM suite. Clients can select modules while sharing a common employee data backbone. Module categories include Core HR, Recruitment, Onboarding, Time and Attendance, Leave, Payroll and Compensation, Travel and Expense, Performance, Talent and Succession, Learning, Employee Engagement Vibe, Rewards and Recognition, People Analytics, Helpdesk, Workforce Management, and Mobile App.

## Core HR
Core HR is the single source of truth for employee data. It supports organization structures, departments, business units, legal entities, employee databases, custom fields, no-code workflows, role-based access control, separation, transfer, promotion, movement workflows, letter generation, document management, org charts, and multi-country, multi-entity, multi-currency support.

## Recruitment ATS
Darwinbox Recruitment is an end-to-end applicant tracking system. It includes manpower planning, requisition workflows, sourcing through job boards, social channels, referrals, agencies, and career sites, AI CV parsing, AI stack ranking, candidate portals, interview scheduling, voice-enabled interview feedback, background verification integrations, digital offer letters, e-signature, candidate communications over email, SMS, and WhatsApp, and hiring analytics such as time-to-hire, cost-per-hire, and source effectiveness.

## Onboarding
Darwinbox Onboarding covers pre-boarding through the first 90 days. It provides a digital pre-boarding portal, document upload checklists, buddy assignment, induction plans, stakeholder task assignment for HR, IT, Admin, Finance and managers, asset issuance, welcome kit logistics, employee code generation, payroll, attendance and leave master updates, handbook sharing, org chart access, probation tracking, and confirmation workflows.

## Time and Attendance
Darwinbox Time and Attendance supports biometric devices, web self-service punch, mobile app punch with geo-tagging and geo-fencing, facial recognition with liveliness checks, and Excel bulk upload. It supports policies for minimum working hours, late-coming grace, early-leaving, break deductions, attendance cycles, holiday calendars, day/night/rotational shifts, regularization workflows, overtime tracking, bulk approvals, voicebot actions, attendance lock for payroll, and reports for attendance summary, late marks, absences, overtime, and exceptions.

## Leave Management
Darwinbox Leave Management supports configurable leave types by geography and entity such as casual leave, sick leave, privilege leave, earned leave, maternity leave, paternity leave, comp-off, sabbatical, and bereavement. It includes accrual rules, carry-forward, encashment, lapse rules, approval hierarchies, auto-approval after timeout, real-time leave balances, calendar views, requests through web, mobile, voicebot, WhatsApp, MS Teams and Slack, and payroll integration for loss of pay and leave encashment.

## Payroll and Compensation
Darwinbox Payroll and Compensation supports a simple four-step payroll process through the RIVeR framework: Review, Initiate, Verify and e-approve, Release and Report. It includes salary structure builder, CTC, gross, net, components, formulas, investment declarations, tax computation, Form 16 generation for India, statutory compliance such as PF, ESI, PT, LWF and TDS in India, loans and advances, reimbursements with OCR bill scanning, final settlement automation, payslips, bank files, multi-country payroll, compensation planning, increment cycles, bonus management, and merit planning.

## Performance Management

It securely connects customer-deployed AI agents with Darwinbox workflows for real-time agent-driven actions such as status updates, approvals, notifications, query-based search, semantic search, predictive analytics, and agentic workflows while preserving security boundaries.

## Integrations and APIs

Common endpoints include employee data fetch, username/password validation, custom SSO token validation, adding standard documents, and adding or updating email, education, and past work details.

## SOP Attendance Management
Attendance SOP: configure minimum working hours, late grace, early leaving, break deductions, cycle dates and holiday calendar; define day, night and rotational shifts and map employees; capture data through biometric, ESS web, geo-fenced mobile or Excel; process regularizations for missed punch, outdoor duty and work from home; review attendance reports; lock attendance for payroll.

## SOP Onboarding Day 1
Onboarding Day 1 checklist: generate employee code, update HRMS, add the employee to payroll, attendance and leave masters, issue welcome kit, ID card and laptop, share induction plan with HoD, reporting manager and new joiner, issue employee handbook and org chart, brief safety and facilities, and upload statutory documents.

## SOP Leave Process
Leave process: employee submits request through web, mobile or voicebot; request routes to managers based on approval hierarchy; manager approves or rejects, or auto-approval happens after timeout; leave balance updates in real time; payroll sync handles LWP and encashment; notifications go to employee via email, push, WhatsApp or Teams.

## SOP Payroll RIVeR
Payroll cycle using RIVeR: Review attendance and payroll inputs such as LOP, overtime, reimbursements, joinings, exits and increments; Initiate payroll calculations; Verify and e-approve by HR and Finance; Release and Report by disbursing, generating payslips and filing statutory returns.

## SOP Performance Review
Performance review cycle: goals are set at the start of the cycle; check-ins, journals and feedback continue through the cycle; optional mid-cycle review; self-appraisal; manager appraisal; multi-stakeholder or 360 inputs; calibration and normalization; final rating and compensation linkage; letter generation and next-cycle goal setting.
